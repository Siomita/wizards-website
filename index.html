The 7-Day Sprint Framework

Most Product Validation Takes Months
Get Your Answer in One Week.

The daily validation system that shows you if people will actually pay you for your idea — before you spend months creating something nobody wants.

Day 01

Pick One. Kill the Rest.

Stop keeping 5 ideas alive as "backup plans." Day 1 forces you to commit to the idea most likely to sell — using a specific scoring method that eliminates wishful thinking.

Day 02

Wallet Research — Find Real Buyers First

Find where your buyers actually hang out online. Learn what language they use, what they're desperately searching for, and what they've already paid for. This is your unfair advantage.

Day 03

Talk to 10 Strangers

You'll send 20 DMs using our exact script. This is how you find out if people actually have the problem you're solving — and what they'd pay to fix it. No audience required.

Day 04

Shape the Offer From Real Feedback

Use what people told you in their own words to build an offer that solves the exact pain they described. Your product's outline comes from your future buyers — not your assumptions.

Day 05

Price It Right — Without Undercharging

Most people price way too low. Day 5 teaches you how to test price points before you commit — so you stop leaving money on the table by charging $10 for something worth $47.

Day 06

Pre-Sell Before You Build

Send a simple pre-sale offer to the people who showed interest. The goal: get at least one person to pay before you've built anything. Money in before a single piece of content is created.

Day 07

Validated or Pivot — Either Way You Win

Review your data. If people paid: you've got a validated product ready to build. If not: you get a clear signal to pivot — saving you months of wasted effort on the wrong idea.

Q: I have zero followers. Will this work for me?

Yes — this is literally built for that. The whole system is designed around talking to strangers in communities where your buyers already hang out: Reddit, X, Discord, TikTok comments. You don't need a following. You need the right conversations.

Q: What if my idea doesn't validate?

That's actually the best outcome — because you find out in 7 days instead of 6 months. The sprint gives you a clear pivot framework so you know exactly what to adjust. A failed validation is free market research. A failed product launch is just expensive failure.

Q: I'm not a "business person." Is this too advanced?

No prior experience needed. Each day has one task and exact scripts to follow. If you can send a DM and read a response, you can run this sprint. The hardest part is committing to one idea on Day 1 — everything else follows a clear path.

Q: How much time does it actually take per day?

About 1–2 hours per day. Some days (like the research days) run a bit longer. But most days have a single focused task that you can complete in a lunch break or an evening. The sprint is designed to fit around your life — not replace it.

Q: What kind of products can I validate with this?

Any digital product — ebooks, online courses, templates, coaching, communities, newsletters, prompt packs, whatever. The validation framework doesn't care what you're building. It's about finding buyers first. The product comes second.

Q: Why is this only $37? Is it actually good?
